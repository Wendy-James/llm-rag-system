# Boundary Statement

This public repository uses sample/public-style documents and does not contain company confidential documents, private JD databases, personal interview records, or proprietary embeddings.

The resume project should be described as a retrieval-evaluation supplement, not a large-model training project.

It does not claim:

- online deployment;
- production ownership;
- private enterprise knowledge-base data;
- model fine-tuning or large-model training.

What I can discuss in an interview:

- corpus and chunk schema;
- query-evidence evaluation set design;
- Recall@K, MRR, NDCG, citation hit rate, and no-answer control;
- chunk-size ablation;
- BM25, dense retrieval, RRF, and reranker tradeoffs;
- hallucination and unsupported-answer badcases.
